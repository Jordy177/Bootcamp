Multiple Linear Regression¶

Simple linear regression can easily be extended to include multiple features. This is called multiple linear regression:. A straight-plane fit is a model of the form

$$ \large y = \large \beta + \large \alpha_1 x_1 + \large \alpha_2 x_2 +...+ \large \alpha_n x_n $$

where:
$\alpha_i$ is commonly known as the slope in multiple dimensions that makes up a plane
$\beta$ is commonly known as the intercept.
